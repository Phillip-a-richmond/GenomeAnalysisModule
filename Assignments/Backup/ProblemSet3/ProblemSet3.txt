#Problem Set 3

## Overview
In this problem set, you will copy a script to map your case data, edit it to be relevant to your case, and then submit to the scheduler.

1. Create a ProblemSet3 directory in your Student workshop directory:

/mnt/scratch/Public/TRAINING/GenomeAnalysisModule/StudentSpaces/<YourDir>/ProblemSet3/

2. Copy this template script to that directory:

/mnt/scratch/Public/TRAINING/GenomeAnalysisModule/Scripts/MapAndConvert_CaseTemplate.sh

3. Edit the template to match your case, and your email address. Double check that all your fastq files are present in your case directory:

/mnt/scratch/Public/TRAINING/GenomeAnalysisModule/Scripts/CaseInformation/CaseFiles/<CaseN>/

4. Submit to the scheduler. 

5. Done!
